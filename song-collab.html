A Night During Spring

Music has been a large part of my life. I was in band since elementary school, starting on the flute and transitioning to percussion (as I sucked at the flute). I personally think that music holds a lot of power and emotion that cannot be as easily expressed in other artistic mediums.

With that in mind, I wanted to make music that I could relax to and enjoyed. I also tend to turn my ambitions into reality! Here is the finished product of the joint song I made with a friend. I would like to go back someday to redo parts of it, though, as we had zero experience when we started.
