Stanford Vietnamese Student Association

I have been a part of Stanford's Vietnamese Student Association (SVSA) for three years now! I held two positions during my time in SVSA: Culture Night Intern and Culture Night Co-Chair. For these roles I helped plan SVSA's largest event of the year come spring: Culture Night.

Despite not being Vietnamese, I built lasting friends and inspiration to help found Stanford's Lao and Laotion Student Union (LLSU). It also led me to serve as a planner for Southeast Asian (SEA/SE Asian) "Mixer" events where many SEA student orgs gather together and participate in a wide range of activities and celebrate our similarities and differences.
